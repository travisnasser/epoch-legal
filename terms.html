Program Description

Epoch AI is a personal AI assistant that sends SMS notifications, reminders, daily briefings, and informational messages to the account owner's registered phone number. This is a single-user notification service — only the account holder who has opted in will receive messages.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your configuration. Typical usage ranges from 1 to 10 messages per day, depending on the notifications and reminders you have enabled. You control the frequency through your assistant settings.

Message and data rates may apply. Standard carrier messaging rates apply to all SMS messages sent and received. Check with your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-In

You opt in to receive SMS messages by configuring your AI assistant with your phone number and enabling SMS notifications. By providing your phone number and activating SMS messaging, you consent to receive text messages from Epoch AI at the number provided.

Opt-Out

You can opt out at any time. To stop receiving messages:

  • Reply STOP to any message to immediately unsubscribe
  • Disable SMS notifications in your assistant configuration

After sending STOP, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.
