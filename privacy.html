Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Overview

Epoch AI ("we", "us", "our") is a personal AI assistant notification service. This privacy policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information related to our SMS messaging service.

Information We Collect

We collect only the following information:

  • Phone number: The mobile phone number you provide to receive SMS notifications.
  • Message history: Records of SMS messages sent to your number for service operation and troubleshooting.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used solely to deliver personal notifications, reminders, and informational messages that you have requested. This includes:

  • Daily briefings (weather, news, calendar)
  • Schedule reminders and alerts
  • Informational updates and summaries

Information Sharing

We do not sell, share, rent, or disclose your personal information to any third parties for marketing or any other purpose. Your phone number and message data are used exclusively for delivering the notification service you have opted into.

Data Retention

Message logs are retained only as long as necessary for service operation. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encrypted transmission of messages via Twilio's secure messaging platform.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request access to your stored data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP to any message
